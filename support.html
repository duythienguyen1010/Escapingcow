Frequently asked questions

How do I play the game?

You can play the game by clicking on the links on the navigation bar or on the homepage, right now there is only one game available for you to play and learn basic Python. But we are planning to do more in the future.

Is the games hard?

The game can be quite challenging if players has no general idea about coding, we would suggest that you use our game as a way to practice and learn new concepts. There are many site that can help you learn the materials such as KhanAcademy.

What do I do if I can't solve an encountered problem?

You can search up the questions or the concepts online to learn more about it. If you still don't understand, please contact us using the link below, we are happy to help and will reach you as soon as possible!
